What Is The Use Of Handwritten Sheets?

These notes are asked to be written in loose sheets. Notes of the same topics are collected from different books of different writers. When written in loose sheets, it makes it easy to edit. Those collected notes are edited and re-published in a new title so that there is no copyright claim.
